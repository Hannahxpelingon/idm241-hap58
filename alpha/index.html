Mailchimp Microinteraction

Button Microinteraction

Mailchimp website

Triggers

  • Hover over the logo
  • Click the logo

Rules

  • On click: When the user taps on the button, the page automatically refreshes

Feedback

  • On hover
    • Light gray box shadow surrounding the logo appears
    • Logo bounces up and down once
    • As the logo bounces, the monkey opens its eyes then returns to the winking (original position)
  • On click
    • A small Intuit tab flashes from the top then disappears once the page is reloaded

Loops: Monkey winking when the user hovers over the button

Mode: None
